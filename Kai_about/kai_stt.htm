State Street Corporation

State Street Corporation (also known as State Street)

is a US-based international financial services holding company. It was founded in 1792 and its headquarters are in Boston, Massachusetts. State Street is organized into three main divisions:

Global Services: The Global Services business is a custodian bank with $28 trillion (USD) of assets under custody and administration.
Global Advisors: The Global Advisors business provides investment management services and has $2.3 trillion (USD) of assets under management.
Global Markets: The Global Markets business offers investment research and trading services to institutional investors.
